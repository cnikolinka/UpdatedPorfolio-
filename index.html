About Me

My name is Nikolina, I grew up in a small town called Pisz, in a lake infested province in Poland. Growing up I had big dreams of becoming a veterinarian one day as I always had a very special place in my heart for animals. My family and I migrated to the states in the Fall of 2003.

I had to learn the language, adapt to the new melting-pot cultural differences, and find new peers. After graduating High School I was nowhere near ready to step into the real world, or to decide what I'd like to study in college. My dreams of becoming a veterinarian were no longer possible due to my immigrational-status conflict.

I tried my strengths in Dietary Science and then a few years later in Nursing. Life has brought difficulties which forced me to quit school both times. While being away from school and supporting myself with my full-time bartending job I knew I needed to gain an education and start a more serious career path. Learning to code was a brand new idea, a scary as much as an exciting one.

signed up for the Rutgers bootcamp diving deep into the unknown. Even though I begin this journey with absolutely no prior experience. I am confident in my ability to learn and persevere.
